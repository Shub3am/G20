Welcome to G-20 Bharat 2023

Our Mission

At G20, our mission is clear: to promote unity, cooperation, and collective action among nations. We firmly believe that the world's most pressing issues, from economic stability and trade to climate change and security, can only be effectively addressed through international collaboration.

Inclusivity

The G20 includes 19 individual countries and the European Union, representing a diverse group of economies from every continent. This inclusivity ensures that a broad range of perspectives is considered in our deliberations.

High-Level Meetings

Our annual Summit consists of Several Ministers and Governors Meeting, and various working groups convene at the highest levels of government and expertise to tackle economic challenges effectively.

Engagement Groups

We engage with various stakeholders, including business, civil society, industrialists, and think tanks, to ensure a well-rounded and inclusive approach to our work.

Digital Inclusion

We strive for digital inclusion, ensuring that the benefits of the digital economy are accessible to all.

Join Us in Shaping the Future

We invite you to become a part of G20's global community and contribute to shaping a more united world.

Whether you are a leader, policymaker, or advocate for positive change, G20 offers a unique opportunity to engage with like-minded individuals and work towards a brighter future for all.

G20's Impact

G20 champions multilateralism as the cornerstone of effective global governance

Over the years, G20 has played a pivotal role in addressing some of the most critical issues facing humanity. From brokering international trade agreements to promoting sustainable development goals, our conferences have led to tangible outcomes that have a positive impact on nations and people worldwide.

Consensus-Based Decision-Making

Throughout its history, the G20 has achieved significant milestones in the pursuit of global economic stability and sustainable development.

The G20 operates on the principle of consensus, meaning that all member nations must agree on decisions and commitments, fostering cooperation and unity.
